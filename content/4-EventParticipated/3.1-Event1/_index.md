# AWS First Cloud AI Journey Community Day 2026

**Date:** Saturday, March 21, 2026 | 09:00 – 12:00  
**Location:** Floor 26, Bitexco Financial Tower, 2 Hai Trieu Street, Ben Nghe Ward, District 1, Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam  
**Role:** Attendee (FCJ Cloud Intern - Team NeuraX)

## Event Description

AWS First Cloud AI Journey Community Day 2026 was a high-energy gathering celebrating the convergence of modern infrastructure and cutting-edge AI applications. The event brought together cloud experts, developers, and technology enthusiasts to explore new applications of Cloud and Generative AI through real-world demos and networking opportunities. The event also marked the official kickoff of the FCAJ Bootcamp 2026, launching a learning and skill development journey for students participating in the program.

## Main Activities

The event featured 5 major technical sessions covering the evolution of cloud engineering and AI:

**Check-in:** 8:30 – 9:00 AM at Floor 26

**Session 1: Modern Platform Engineering & Career Pathways** (09:00 - 09:45 AM)  
Discover company culture, internship opportunities, and how to engage with speakers through an interactive Q&A platform. Get an introduction to Platform Engineering and its role in modern cloud and DevOps ecosystems. Join an open Q&A session to ask questions and gain insights directly from industry experts.

**Session 2: GenAIOps Essential - DevOps for Generative AI Applications** (09:45 - 10:15 AM)  
DevOps Fundamentals on AWS and Learning Resources. Real-world examples of GenAIOps for AWS Projects with Bedrock AgentCore, Observability, EKS, and Langfuse.

**Session 3: Shipping Code in the Agentic Era** (10:15 – 10:45 AM)  
Exploring the problem, tools, and deep dive into the Productivity Playbook for the agentic era. Includes a live demo of shipping code with AI assistance.

**Break:** 10:45 - 11:00 AM

**Session 4: Production-Grade Multimodal GenAI on AWS** (11:00 – 11:30 AM)  
The New AI Application Stack, Multimodal Search with Nova Embeddings, GraphRAG for Enterprise Knowledge, Multi-Agent Workflows, and Safe & Observable GenAI.

**Session 5: From Edge To Origin: CloudFront as Your Foundation** (11:30 - 12:00 PM)  
Amazon CloudFront for every workload, cost optimization, security capabilities, enhanced reliability, and enhanced performance.

## Outcomes

- **Platform Engineering as Foundation:** Understanding that modern cloud systems require a solid platform engineering foundation before scaling AI applications. This directly aligns with Lexi's architecture using AWS SAM and infrastructure-as-code.
- **GenAIOps Best Practices:** Learned how to operate GenAI systems in production using AWS services (Bedrock, EKS, Langfuse) with proper observability and optimization. Directly applicable to Lexi's backend infrastructure.
- **AI-Driven Productivity:** Recognized the shift in how engineers work—leveraging AI tools to increase productivity and solve more complex problems efficiently. Relevant for Lexi's development workflow.
- **Multimodal AI at Scale:** Gained insights into building production-grade multimodal AI systems using Nova Embeddings, GraphRAG, and multi-agent architectures. Potential future enhancements for Lexi.
- **CloudFront Optimization:** Learned about Amazon CloudFront for cost optimization, security, reliability, and performance. Applicable to Lexi's frontend deployment strategy.
- **Community Connection:** The event reinforced the importance of community collaboration in advancing Vietnam's AI ecosystem and the official kickoff of FCAJ Bootcamp 2026.
- **Application to Lexi:** The sessions on GenAIOps, Multimodal AI, and CloudFront directly informed Lexi's architecture decisions around using Bedrock for AI features, proper observability with logging, scalable multi-agent patterns, and CDN optimization for frontend delivery.
- **Career Growth:** The Platform Engineering and Career Pathways session provided valuable perspective on long-term skill development in the cloud and AI space, reinforcing the importance of continuous learning.
